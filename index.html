Arun Durvasula

I'm Arun Durvasula, a PhD student at UCLA in the Department of Human Genetics working with Kirk Lohmueller and Sriram Sankararaman. I study the forces that shape and maintain genetic variation. Previously, I worked in Angela Hancock's lab at the Max Planck Institute for Plant Breeding Research studying demography in Arabidopsis thaliana. Prior to that, I worked in Jeff Ross-Ibarra's lab  studying demography in maize, developing analysis tools, and looking at crop-wild introgression in Oryza at UC Davis. I study genetics, statistics, and computer science. You can find me on the web in the following places:
